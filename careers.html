FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a CS/IT degree mandatory?
No. We hire based on skills, projects, and problem-solving ability — not degrees. If you can build, we want you. We've hired exceptional engineers from non-CS backgrounds, bootcamps, and self-taught paths.
Are remote positions fully remote?
Yes, positions marked "Remote" are fully remote from anywhere in India. Hybrid roles require 2-3 days/week at our Amethi office. We use Slack, Zoom, and Notion for async collaboration.
What's the interview process like?
5 steps: Application → HR Screening (20 min) → Technical Round (60-90 min, real problems) → Culture Round (30 min with leadership) → Offer. Total timeline: 7-10 business days. No trick questions, no whiteboard hazing.
Do interns get paid?
Yes! ₹10K-25K/month depending on skills and role. Interns work on real production projects (not PowerPoint presentations). Strong performers get PPO within 3 months.
What tech stack will I work with?
Depends on your role — Data Engineers use Databricks, Kafka, PySpark, Airflow, Snowflake. Web devs use React, Next.js, Node.js, Python. AI engineers use LangChain, OpenAI, PyTorch. DevOps uses AWS, Kubernetes, Terraform. We invest in the latest tools.
What certifications do you sponsor?
AWS Solutions Architect, AWS Data Engineer, Databricks Data Engineer, GCP Professional, Terraform Associate, and more. Full exam fee + preparation material covered. Plus ₹50K annual learning budget for courses, books, and conferences.
