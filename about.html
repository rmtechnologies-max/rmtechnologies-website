About RM Technologies

Born from a ₹20Cr+ FMCG empire. Built to power the next generation of enterprise technology.

Who We Are

Enterprise Tech.
Rooted in Real Business.

RM Technologies is the technology subsidiary of Rajesh Masala Udyog Private Limited, a leading FMCG manufacturer based in Amethi, Uttar Pradesh. We were built to solve real business problems — managing 5000+ vendor relationships, processing 1–2 million daily order transactions, and supporting a ₹10–20 Crore annual revenue operation.

What started as an internal tech team evolved into a fully independent technology company. Our engineers built Medallion Architecture on AWS Databricks, Kafka-based ingestion pipelines, real-time ETL workflows, and AI-powered analytics systems — all in production, all at scale.

Today, we bring this same battle-tested expertise to external enterprises across India and beyond — offering data engineering, cloud solutions, web development, AI/ML, and IT consulting services.

Our Journey

From FMCG to
Enterprise Tech

Every great tech company has an origin story. Ours starts not in a Silicon Valley garage, but in the heart of Amethi, UP — inside one of India's growing FMCG enterprises.

The complexity of managing 5000+ vendors, coordinating multi-channel orders, and driving data-backed decisions demanded technology that simply didn't exist off the shelf. So we built it ourselves.

2020

Rajesh Masala Udyog Founded

Rajesh Masala Udyog Private Limited (CIN: U15490UP2020PTC127010) incorporated on Feb 13, 2020 in Amethi, UP. FMCG manufacturing operations begin.

2021

Internal Tech Team Formed

First engineers hired to handle vendor management, order processing systems, and basic data infrastructure. The seed of RM Technologies is planted.

2022

Vendor Management System Built

Custom vendor portal and mobile app launched for 5000+ vendors. REST API + Kafka ingestion pipelines operational. Data Engineering team expanded.

2023

Medallion Architecture Deployed

End-to-end Medallion Architecture (Bronze/Silver/Gold) on AWS Databricks goes live. PySpark transformations, Airflow orchestration, Delta Lake optimization. 1–2M orders/day processed reliably.

2024

RM Technologies — Independent Launch

RM Technologies officially launches as an independent tech company under the parent FMCG group. AI-powered analytics assistant deployed. CI/CD workflows automated.

2025

Taking External Enterprise Projects

RM Technologies opens doors to external clients. Full-service offerings: Data Engineering, Web Dev, AI/ML, Cloud & DevOps, IT Consulting. Growing team. Global ambitions.
