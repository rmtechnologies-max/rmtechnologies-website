About Us

Born in FMCG.
Built for Enterprise Tech.

RM Technologies started as the internal tech division of Rajesh Masala Udyog Private Limited — a leading FMCG company manufacturing masala and food products in Amethi, Uttar Pradesh.

What began as managing 5000+ vendor relationships and processing 1–2 million daily order transactions evolved into a full-scale enterprise technology company. Today, we bring that same battle-tested expertise to external clients across industries.
